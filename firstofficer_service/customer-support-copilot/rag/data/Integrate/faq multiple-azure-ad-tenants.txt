# Can Atlan integrate with multiple Azure AD tenants within a single instance?

Atlan can currently only connect to any one Azure AD tenant.
However, you canreach out to Atlan supportto share more details about your organizational setup and requirements. For example, are your users currently assigned to separate tenants? This will help us better understand your use case.
